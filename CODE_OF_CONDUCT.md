# Code of Conduct

We are committed to building an open, respectful, and inclusive community. By participating in Stellar-Provenance, you agree to uphold this code.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful, considerate, and constructive.
- Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels.
- Provide helpful feedback; focus on ideas, not individuals.
- Use appropriate channels and keep content suitable for all audiences.
- No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or spam.

## Expected Behavior
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.
- Keep discussions on-topic and in the correct channels.
- Offer and receive feedback gracefully.
- Credit the work and ideas of others.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Threats or encouragement of violence or self-harm.
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances.
- Doxxing or sharing others' private information.
- Spamming, trolling, or derailment of discussions.

## Scope
This code applies to all project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, community chats (Discord/Matrix), events, and any public or private communication related to the project.

## Reporting
- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please email the maintainers at `security@stellar-provenance.dev` or open a confidential issue labeled `conduct`.
- For urgent matters in chat spaces, contact moderators directly.

## Enforcement
- Maintainers and moderators may take any appropriate action, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from community spaces.
- Repeated or severe violations may result in expulsion from the project and related communities.
